Chicken Adobo

Chicken adobo is a classic Filipino dish. Enjoy this easy recipe with your loved ones with just a few ingredients, including garlic, bay leaf, black pepper, and soy sauce.

Caldereta

Caldereta is a dish you must try. Meats and vegetables are braised in a tomato sauce to create this hearty dish. It's a quick and easy meal to make at home.

Kare-Kare

Kare-Kare is a famous Filipino dish that consists of strips of beef cooked in peanut sauce. Learn how to make it. The recipe is quick and easy to cook.
